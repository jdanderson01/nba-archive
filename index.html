Welcome to the NBA Archive!

The NBA archive is a project focused on highlighting past games that are deemed noteworthy, whether it's a single game or career achievement, an improbable playoff series win, or a heated competition All-Star Weekend.

More info

Dunk Contests

Review highlights and photos from the most iconic All-Star Weekend Dunk Contests throughout the years, including Clyde Drexler vs Michael Jordan and Vince Carter's famous show-stopper.

Big Time Shots

Relive the moments leading up to and after some of the biggest shots taken and made in NBA history, playoff and regular season!

Wild West

Go through some of the most competitive Western Conference series of the 2000s, including Steve Nash's "7 seconds or less" Suns vs the Spurs Dynasty and controversial Kings vs Lakers series.

Fierce Rivalries

Explore some of the NBA's fiercest rivalries throughout the years such as Celtics v Lakers, Pistons v Bulls and the Warriors v Cavaliers. These also include famous player rivalries like Jordan v Thomas and Bird v Magic.

European Stars

In honor of the Eurobasket Tournament going on, take a look at all of the European players who blazed the way for the European stars today and the techniques they brought to the game.

Forgotten Teams

Learn more about forgotten NBA teams that had a potential to be dynasties and mark their place as all-time teams in the league. Pictured above are the 1980s Houston Rockets featuring the "Twin Towers" of Hakeem Olajuwon and Ralph Sampson.
